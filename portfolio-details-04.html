The 'BlackJack'

This project represents the implementation of the classic game of 21 (Blackjack) in Ruby.

The following mechanics and rules of the game are implemented: Presence of a player (user) and a dealer (controlled by the program). Initial game bank for the player and the dealer - $100. Dealing two random cards to the user, which they can see, and two cards to the "dealer," hidden from the user. Counting the total points for each player. Player's turn with options to stand, hit, or reveal cards. Automatic dealer's turn depending on the total points. Revealing cards and counting game results. Ending the game with the option to start a new game or finish the current one.
